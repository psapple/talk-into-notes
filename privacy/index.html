Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-08

Talk into Notes is designed to keep your data private.

App data

Talk into Notes records audio that you choose to create and processes transcripts on your device. I do not operate a backend server that receives or stores your recordings or transcripts.

Apple Notes export

If you choose to export content to Apple Notes, that export happens through Apple’s system features on your device. Your notes are then handled by Apple Notes and your iCloud / device setup.

Support email

If you contact support, I receive your email address and any information you include in your message. I use that information only to respond to your request and troubleshoot the issue you reported.

Website hosting

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may process technical information such as IP address and standard web request data for security and site delivery.

Third parties

This site does not use analytics, advertising, tracking pixels, or contact forms.
